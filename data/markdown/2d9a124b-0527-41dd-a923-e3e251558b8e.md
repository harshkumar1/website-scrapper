September 7, 2022 0 151 Event Date: September 7, 2022 Location T-HUB E-Mail support-startup@telangana.gov.in Titan Capital’s ChaiWithTitan in partnership with THub invites you for a candid conversation with Bipin Shah, Partner – Titan Capital & entrepreneur Kiran Naik, Co-Founder – VEGROW as they demystify the fundamentals of raising early money and speak about how they tied the investment knot! Register for the FiresideChat, https://bit.ly/3R28QM4. Early stage funding is no less than a marriage. You need to have the right proposal made to the right partner and strike the right chords to ensure it goes a long way. Hear from two sides in the house, their perspective on building up a perfect pitch & relationship. Explore the key aspects of raising funds during the Fireside Chat on “Early Stage Funding Demystified” on 7th Sept, 4:30 pm at T-Hub with stellar speakers: Bipin Shah: He is the Partner of Titan Capital and spends most of his time hustling with portfolio founders and supporting them in solving company level real problems. Before joining Titan Capital in 2015, he was a founding team member of Lead Angels Network, a seed stage investment platform. Few of their portfolio startups include UrbanCompany, Ola, Razorpay, Mamaearth, Beardo & many others. Kiran Naik : Co-Founder of VEGROW – one of the fastest growing startups in the AgriTech space based out of Hyderabad. VEGROW has raised $40Mn since its incorporation in 2020. It is India’s first AgriTech platform partnering with farmers to redefine and organize their supply, as well as standardize the supply chain through strategic partnerships. Moderated by Srinivas Rao Mahankali (MSR): MSR is the CEO of T-Hub, and leads its mission of driving results and collaboration for entrepreneurs’ success.
