times Admin_StartupTS November 5, 2022 0 153 Event Date: November 5, 2022 Location T-HUB E-Mail support-startup@telangana.gov.in Building a startup is a demanding endeavor in the best of times. Startups encounter various challenges – a search for the right product-market fit, access to capital, technology choices, and building a winning team. Many startups can think through these, make informed choices, execute their strategy to surmount these challenges, and become more sustainable enterprises. Entrepreneurs can do this because they typically have good instincts and insight into resolving challenges relating to their businesses.
