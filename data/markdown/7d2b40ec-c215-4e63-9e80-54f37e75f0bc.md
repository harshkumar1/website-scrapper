support-startup@telangana.gov.in RICH in partnership with Dr. Reddy’s Institute of Life Sciences and the Royal Society of Chemistry organised an online conclave to help the chemical industry achieve its UN Sustainable Development Goals (UN SDGs) that serves as a blueprint to achieve a better and more sustainable future for the world.
