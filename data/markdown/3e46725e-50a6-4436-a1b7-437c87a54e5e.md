Department of Welfare & Senior Citizens, Department of Women & Child Development DCW, and Sri Vishnu Educational Society organized the 3rd edition of Telangana Assistive Technology Exhibition on 3rd December, 2022 at T-Hub Hyderabad, commemorating the International Day for Persons with Disability and had exposed 39 innovations devised by startups, innovators, National Institutes, and students from BVRIT Hyderabad devised to solve the problems being faced by Children with Special Needs (CWSN), elderly, injured and PWDs.
