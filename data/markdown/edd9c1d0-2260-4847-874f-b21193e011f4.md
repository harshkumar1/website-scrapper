support-startup@telangana.gov.in Telangana State Innovation Cell (TSIC) in collaboration with Welfare Department and Sarva Shiksha Abhiyan-Telangana and Giftolexia, a startup in the space of learning difficulties, organised a workshop on ‘Innovative and inclusive classroom strategies for children with specific learning difficulties’. The workshop was attended by about 430 educators and inclusive education resource persons (IERPs) from across Telangana.
