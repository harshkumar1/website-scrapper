Admin_StartupTS September 27, 2022 0 139 Event Date: September 27, 2022 Location T-HUB E-Mail support-startup@telangana.gov.in THub’s recent edition of CommunityConnect was one to remember for the StartupEcosystem. A heartfelt thanks to 300+ founders who engaged in the star-packed session for 3 hours, which is about an evening of 1000+ hours of high-quality value addition. Amit Kumar Agarwal (NoBroker.com) emphasised the importance of a “never say die attitude” in a founder and shared how #NoBroker grew from being rejected by 1000+ investors to a unicorn. Taran Chhabra (Neeman’s Shoes) bared the truth on the importance of building a sustainable business model around a cause while being candid about his struggle during the initial days of his journey. Prabhdeep Singh (StanPlus) drew from a personal event in his life to be the main motivation behind coming up with his impact startup, which is today one of India’s largest online ambulance aggregators. He emphasised how if the business model is not robust, one can never create sustainable and long-term impact.
