AWS | Intel Innovation Pod for Drones is a program powered by Amazon Web Services and Intel. This program was in collaboration with T-Hub and Telangana State Innovation Cell (TSIC), which received over 300+ applications from all across India. The idea of the program is to create pilots for the startups with the Ecosystem Partners like Ag-Hub, Cyberabad Police, Roads & Buildings Department, Government of Telangana, AGI and World Economic Forum (WEF). The ten selected had received opportunities to pitch in front of the Ecosystem Partners for possible paid / unpaid pilots in various use cases like Agri, Surveillance, Mapping and more. The Startups in this program had direct access to AWS Startup Ramp Program, which disbursed AWS Credits up to $100,000. Based on their requirement, the Startups have received mentorship in the areas of Sales Conversion, Design Development, Financial Modelling etc. The Meet will give the #founders a chance to interact with fellow DroneTech Startup Founders, AWS Team and T-Hub Team to find some synergies and network. 3764 Views Recent Events August 24, 2022 AgHub hosted Agri Incubator Connect in Association with ThinkAg A-gub, PJTSAU Read More June 30, 2022 NASSCOM GCC Conclave – Start-Up Pitching Sessions Virtual Read More Contact Details Telangana Innovation Cell, Cabin 17, 3rd Floor, T-Hub 2.0, 20, Inorbit Mall Rd, Vittal Rao Nagar, Madhapur, Telangana 500081.
