support-startup@telangana.gov.in To nurture the culture of design thinking and innovation in the schools of Telangana, TSIC in collaboration with the Department of School Education, National Innovation Foundation, UNICEF and Inqui-Lab Foundation, had facilitated a 2-day residential workshop for the 33 District Science Officers (DSOs) of Telangana on 20th and 21st of September, 2022 at TSIRD.
