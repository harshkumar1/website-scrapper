By T-Hub Women in Manufacturing, By We-Hub Fintech Arcade program is a first-of-its-kind Speed Dating process between fintech startups and identified Corporates that focuses on exploring strategic partnerships to ease the process of payments, financial inclusion, tax management etc. The process of engagement can vary from subscribing to the startups products/services to being onboarded as vendors. WE Hub Partnered with corporates to work with their Diversity and Inclusion initiatives.
