support-startup@telangana.gov.in On July 16th, Telangana State Innovation Cell (TSIC), along with T-Hub, in collaboration with Startup India has organised Hyderabad Edition of Drone Startup Meetup. The meetup was conducted to sensitise all the drone-related startups in Telangana on NavIC Grand Challenge by DPIIT and ISRO. The meetup, which witnessed a footfall of 150+ entrepreneurs from drone sector, had interaction on the application process for the grand challenge.
