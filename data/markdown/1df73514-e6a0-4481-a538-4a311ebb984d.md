On 23rd September 2021, Telangana State Innovation Cell, ITE&C Department, Government of Telangana as a part of “Azadi ka Amrit Mahotsav”, celebrated 75 years of Independence on the theme “Social Impact,” the state has been pioneering with its Inclusive Ecosystem. The event that saw the celebration of successful Innovators, Entrepreneurs, Leaders in the State through panels and success stories, saw the presence of Shri.
