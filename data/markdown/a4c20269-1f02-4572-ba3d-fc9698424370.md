Hyderabad has evolved as one of the most vibrant cities in India. An ideal location for Corporations to expand their services, while nurturing an inclusive culture of Co-working for entrepreneurs, freelancers, and business owners. The city has seen an explosion of emerging companies and Startups creating a demand for Co-working spaces. At present, the state has 78 incubators, accelerators, and coworking spaces with specific focus on various sectors such as Lifesciences, FinTech, Agri Tech, Digital etc. The Government has also stepped up its efforts to support the needs of the Startups and emerging companies for coworking spaces.
