INCUBATION SUPPORT TO STARTUPS Overview: An incubator is an organizational setup that supports, provides, and nurtures the creative, innovative startups to scale up its operations by providing mentorship, training, market access, and working space. Each incubator may be varied in their operational, business, and functional strategies. Incubators provide a cohesive working environment for startups through physical spaces or virtual incubation opportunities. The Government of Telangana over the years have supported the establishment of various incubators in the state. The support spans across providing capital expenditure, operational expenditure, land, and other form of support. Government has also focused actively on creating incubators in the Higher Educational Institutions to support student entrepreneurs. Given below is the list of state supported Incubators: SI.
